FAQ

How quickly can I start participating?
Provisioning typically completes within days after KYC verification and contract signing.
Can I confirm infrastructure fit before committing budget?
Yes. You can confirm the quote and configuration details before you sign and pay.
What happens if servers are down?
We have GPU Services SLA with clear, accountable uptime — compensated if breached. This SLA applies to infrastructure availability only: network-level downtime, missed rewards, or any "mining/participation" outcomes are not covered.
How does billing work?
  • Month-to-month contracts
  • Payment for the first month is required before server assignment
  • Renewal: pay no later than 10 days before contract end
  • Monthly invoices are issued at calendar month end
Can I scale up or down?
Upgrade is available anytime (subject to capacity). Downgrade is available once per month.
Do you offer discounts?
Volume discounts can be discussed when ordering multiple servers.
What support is available?
Business hours support for infrastructure issues, with clear next steps and update cadence.
